# Safety and scope

AMP-JEPA-Hybrid v3 is for computational peptide discovery research. The outputs are candidate hypotheses, not validated therapeutics.

The pipeline does not provide wet-lab protocols, culture conditions, dosing guidance, or clinical recommendations.

Recommended downstream evaluation should prioritize benign, standard antimicrobial-peptide characterization endpoints such as predicted activity, hemolysis, cytotoxicity, solubility, novelty, and stability before any experimental decisions.
